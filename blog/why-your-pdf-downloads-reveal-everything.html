üé≠ Method 1: Invisible Web Beacons

Modern PDF tracking systems embed invisible 1x1 pixel images that load from remote servers when you open the document. These "web beacons" instantly transmit your information to tracking databases.

How It Works:

  1. PDF contains hidden image elements
  2. Your PDF reader attempts to load these images
  3. Request sent to tracking server with your IP
  4. Server logs all your information

üîó Method 2: Smart Link Tracking

Instead of direct PDF files, you're given links that appear normal but route through sophisticated tracking systems.

The Process:

  • Click on "PDF download" link
  • Redirected through tracking infrastructure
  • Your data collected in milliseconds
  • Finally served the actual PDF

üåä Method 3: Dynamic Watermarking

  • Hidden tracking codes in document metadata
